Froots is free forever when you bring your own model access — no account, no trial clock, nothing rented. Everything lives on your machine. **Sync** is the one paid add-on: an end-to-end encrypted cloud copy of your workspace so multiple devices stay in step, with memory snapshots and publishing. It's **$12/month** (or $99/year — about 31% off), it's off by default, and you can turn it on during setup or later in **Settings → Plan & billing**. Beta pricing is locked in for life, and you can cancel anytime.

This page is an outline — the full guide is being written. It will cover:

- Turning Sync on and signing in on a second device
- What is (and isn't) encrypted and stored in the cloud
- Memory snapshots and publish
- Managing your subscription, invoices, and cancellation

Current plans and pricing live on the [Pricing](/pricing) page.
